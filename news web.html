Tamil Nadu Schools Gear Up for Inclusive ‘Kalai Thiruvizha’ Cultural Festival

The Tamil Nadu government has announced the “Kalai Thiruvizha” cultural festival for government schools from November 25–28, with different districts hosting various grade levels. For the first time, children from special schools and students with disabilities will also participate, encouraging inclusive engagement in cultural activities alongside academics.

The festival aims to boost student talent, participation, and community engagement across regions. Officials hope it will help develop well-rounded students and provide less-privileged children with broader opportunities beyond academics.
